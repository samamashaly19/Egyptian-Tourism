Egypt History

The history of Egypt is deeply rooted in human history. Thus, the Egyptian people created and rendered the world one of the ancient great civilization. Unique as the Egyptian civilization was in innovation, architecture and arts, it thrilled the world and scholars in thought and science. Hence, it was a consistent civilization that shaped the minds and feelings of the Egyptians. In ancient times, Egypt was the first country worldwide that had been familiar with principles of writing and had invented the hieroglyphic signs and characters. The Ancient Egyptians were keen on recording their history and the events that took place at that time. Against such background, the Egyptians made a leap that transferred Egypt from the pre-historic age to be the first country worldwide to have a written history and an established system. For this purpose, Egypt had become the cradle of all civilizations.  .

Egypt Location

The Egyptian Arab Republic is located in the northeastern corner of the continent of Africa, bordered to the north by the Mediterranean Sea and to the east by the Red Sea and the Gulfs of Suez and Aqaba. The importance of Egypt’s location has emerged since ancient times. Its location has contributed to making it an eastern gateway to the Arab world and it has become a center for trade routes between Africa and Europe..
